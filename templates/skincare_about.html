About SkinCare AI

Our Mission

SkinCare AI is dedicated to making personalized skincare recommendations accessible to everyone. We combine advanced artificial intelligence, computer vision, and dermatological knowledge to analyze your skin and recommend products that address your specific needs.

How It Works

Skin Analysis Technology

Our AI uses advanced computer vision algorithms powered by Google's Gemini AI to analyze your skin from a photo. The system examines multiple aspects of your skin including:

  • Skin type classification (dry, oily, combination, normal)
  • Detection of specific concerns (acne, redness, etc.)
  • Analysis of texture and hydration levels
  • Identification of sensitive areas

Personalized Recommendations

After analyzing your skin, our AI matches your profile to a database of skincare products and ingredients. We recommend products based on:

  • Effectiveness for your specific skin type
  • Ingredients that address your concerns
  • Product compatibility with sensitive skin (if applicable)
  • Science-backed formulations and ingredients

Local Store Mapping

Finding products is easy with our integrated mapping system. By sharing your location, you can:

  • Find nearby stores that carry recommended products
  • Get directions to the closest retailers
  • Discover specialty skincare boutiques
  • Identify pharmacies and beauty stores in your area

Our Technology Stack

Google Gemini AI

Advanced multimodal AI model that processes and analyzes skin images with high accuracy

Folium Maps

Interactive mapping library for visualizing nearby store locations

Geoapify API

Location intelligence platform for finding nearby beauty and health retail stores

Our application is built using Flask, a lightweight Python web framework, combined with modern frontend technologies like Tailwind CSS for a beautiful, responsive user experience.

Privacy & Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your data:

  • Your skin images are processed securely and are not stored longer than needed for analysis
  • Location data is only used to find nearby stores and is not shared with third parties
  • We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or other companies
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
