About AI Cosmetics

Our Mission

At AI Cosmetics, we're on a mission to revolutionize how you find skincare products. We believe everyone deserves personalized skincare recommendations based on their unique skin profile, not generic advice.

How It Works

Advanced AI Analysis

Our proprietary AI engine analyzes your skin photo to identify your skin type, concerns, and needs. It examines factors such as texture, tone, hydration levels, and sensitivity markers.

Product Matching

We match your skin profile against our database of thousands of skincare products, considering ingredients, formulations, and effectiveness for specific skin concerns.

Localized Recommendations

Our location-based service helps you find where to purchase your recommended products nearby, saving you time and hassle.

Continuous Learning

Our AI continuously improves as more users provide feedback on their results, making our recommendations increasingly accurate over time.

Our Technology

AI Cosmetics leverages cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of skin images across diverse skin types, tones, and conditions.

Our AI can identify over 30 different skin characteristics and match them to the most beneficial ingredients and product formulations. We continually update our product database to ensure you get the latest and most effective recommendations.

Privacy & Data Security

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your data:

  • Images are processed securely and not shared with third parties
  • Your skin data is anonymized when used for AI training
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • SSL encryption protects all data transfer
